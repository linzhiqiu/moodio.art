12 FAQ

Questions
you probably have.

How is Moodio different from Runway, Sora, or Seedance?

Those are generation models. Moodio is a production workflow. We cover the entire pipeline ideation, pre-production, production, post-production, and export in one agent that learns your studio's taste. Generation is one step of five, and we can route to any of those models inside the workflow.

Who owns what I create inside Moodio?

You do. Full commercial rights for all output. Your uploaded references, brand assets, and exported projects are yours we don't claim ownership and we don't train foundation models on your private assets.

Can I upload my own brand's reference library or style assets?

Yes. Brand teams and studios upload their reference corpus once; Moodio indexes it with the same structured retrieval we use on the public cinematic corpus. Every project from that point inherits your visual language.

Can my whole team collaborate in real-time?

Yes. Moodio runs on a Figma-style canvas with multi-cursor editing, role-based access, and shared asset libraries. Managers allocate generation credits across the team, and every member's interactions contribute to the agent's learned studio taste.
